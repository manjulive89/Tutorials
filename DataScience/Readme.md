## CRISP DM
Is a Process framework based on Scientific Methods

* Define the problem
* Get Data and information & Quality Assessment
  - When
  - What
  - Why
  - Who
  - Where
  - How
* Construct a Hypothesis
* Test the Hypothesis and Draw Conclusion
* Share the finding

### 6 Phases of CRISP DM
* Business Understaanding
* Data Understaanding
* Data Prepration
* Modeling
* Evaluation
* Deployment
